Our Classroom Culture — Leaders of Tomorrow

This classroom cultivates responsibility, empathy, leadership, teamwork and creativity. Below are the core values and the activities that bring them to life.

Responsibility & Ownership

Students take charge of class events, lead decoration and manage Saturday demonstrations — learning ownership through practice.

Representative activities: Diwali/Republic Day decoration, student-led demos, classroom upkeep.

Empathy & Service

Students design projects that address community needs. Empathy is central — projects consider the real challenges people face.

Representative activities: Be The Change projects, Bridge of Care, community need-mapping and mentor feedback sessions.

Leadership & Initiative

Students run quizzes, lead weekend demonstrations and organize events — building public speaking and organisational skills.

Representative activities: Saturday demos, current-affairs quizzes, festival coordination.

Teamwork & Resilience

Team sports and tournaments teach collaboration, handling setbacks, respect for officials and composure under pressure.

Representative activities: Varsha Nagar Premier League, team training, IPL visits.

Creativity & Curiosity

Art, clay modelling and museum visits encourage creative thinking — connecting arts with STEM and nurturing curiosity.

Representative activities: Drawing, clay modelling, Museum of Solutions trips, project prototyping.
