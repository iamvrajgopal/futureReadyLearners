Our Classroom Culture — Leaders of Tomorrow

A classroom where students learn to take responsibility, to care for community, to lead their peers, collaborate in teams and create with curiosity. Below are five values we cultivate and the activities that reflect them.

Responsibility

Students manage events, decorations and Saturday sessions.

Empathy & Service

Projects that focus on community needs and social impact.

Leadership & Initiative

Students lead quizzes, events and demos.

Teamwork & Resilience

Sports & tournaments build team spirit and composure.

Creativity & Curiosity

Art, museum visits, tinkering and prototype work.

Responsibility & Ownership

Students take charge of class events (Diwali / Republic Day), manage resources and lead Saturday demo sessions — learning ownership through practice.

  • Event decoration & planning
  • Weekly student-led demonstrations
  • Peer-support for scholarship coaching

Empathy & Service

Students design projects thinking about community needs — building prototypes that help displaced families and vulnerable people.

  • AI/ML projects with social impact
  • Community need mapping
  • Presentations to mentors for feedback

Leadership & Initiative

Students lead quizzes, plan events, run demos and manage teams — the classroom is a place to learn public speaking, organization and responsibility.

  • Saturday demo sessions
  • Current affairs quizzes
  • Festival organization

Teamwork & Resilience

The Varsha Nagar Premier League and sports exposure teach teamwork, handling setbacks and composure under pressure.

  • Inter-division tournament (VNPL)
  • Team building & match etiquette
  • Outdoor visits to IPL matches for exposure

Creativity & Curiosity

Art, clay modelling and museum trips cultivate creative thinking and connect the arts with science.

  • Drawing & clay modelling sessions
  • Museum of Solutions visits
  • Tinkering & prototype building
